Our Story

Over a Century of Community Commerce

Since 1914, Smithfield Implement Co. has been the cornerstone of Cache Valley's shopping experience. What started as a humble implement store has grown into a beloved, multi-department destination. Still family-focused, still community-driven, and still committed to giving you more for your money.
